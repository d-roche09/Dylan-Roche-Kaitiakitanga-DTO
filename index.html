What is Kaitiakitanga?

Kaitiakitanga is a Māori word, that means "Guardianship of Natural Resources." Guardianship is another way of saying, protection, so Kaitiakitanga is the protection of natural resources. We, as the people who live among nature and other animals, are expected to be "Kaitiaki" also known as guardians.

Why is it Important

Everything that we have has come from the natural world around us. If we choose to let the environment suffer from our choices we wouldn't be just hurting other animals but ourselves as well. But we can improve the condition of the environment, fix our mistakes.

Kauri Dieback is one of these mistakes.
